# Zenarmor NGFW Lab
### In this lab, I ...
  - Installed Zenarmor on the OPNSense Firewall via the firewall's GUI
  - Explored the Dashboard, Zero Trust Networks, Reports, Live Sessions, Policies, and Settings of the Zenarmor pluggin
  - Due to the free version of Zenarmor being used for this lab and most editing features requiring a subscription, the primary focus of this lab was becoming acquainted with the features of the Next Generation Firewall Zenarmor
